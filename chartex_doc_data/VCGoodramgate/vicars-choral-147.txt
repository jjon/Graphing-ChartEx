vicars-choral-147
Grant by Gilbert de Coupeland, confirmed with his seal, to Robert de Wintonia precentor of York, for a payment of 17 marks, of land in Gothrumgate, as in 146 (reading Warthyl for Warthill); paying Nicholas Ramkil and his heirs 3s. on behalf of Gilbert and his heirs, at Pentecost and Martinmas, the heirs of John son of Isaac 1/2d. at Christmas, and Gilbert and his heirs 1/2d. at Christmas. Warranty. [1235 x 1240] SOURCE: VC 3/1/1, fo. 18. DATE: When Robert de Winchester was precentor (see 532 note). NOTE: See 146.
