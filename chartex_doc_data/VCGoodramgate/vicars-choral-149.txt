vicars-choral-149
Grant, in pure alms, by John de Fetherstan to the vicars and their successors of a rent charge of 1 mark payable at Pentecost and Martinmas, which the executors of Robert de Winton' once precentor of York gave him for his service from a house in Gutherumgat' which Robert the cook and his wife Eve and their heirs or assigns had at fee farm from Robert; paying the fabric of the church of York 12d., at Pentecost and Martinmas. Warranty. Seal. [1236 x 1240] SOURCE: VC 3/1/1, fo. 18v. DATE: After Robert de Winchester's death (see 532 note). NOTE: See 146.
