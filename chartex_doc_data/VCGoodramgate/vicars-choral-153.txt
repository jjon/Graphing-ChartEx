vicars-choral-153
To all faithful in Christ who will see and hear the present text, Adam chaplain son of Robert the hatter wish well in God. Notification that I have appointed Lady Alicia de Nevill, who was the wife of Henry de Nevill and her heirs or assigns as attorneys of my heirs or assigns, to pay yearly to Hugh de Selby and his heirs 2s and 6d, half at Pentecost and half at the feast of St Martin in the winter, for a messuage of land with appurtenances in Goodramgate that I held of Ada the fisherman, brother of Richard the palmer, as it is fully written in the charter that I had from the said Adam about the said messuage. In witness of that I have impressed my seal on the present charter. Witnesses: John de Warthill, Robert de Skegnesse, Peter de Barneby, Galfrid de Queldric, William Malesover, Roger clerk of the Lord Archbishop of York, Adam Punchardu, John de Ogleforth, William vintner, Robert de Moonkgate, Adam de Wycton, John de Leeds, clerk and many others.
