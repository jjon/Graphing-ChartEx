vicars-choral-446
Quitclaim by John Warde citizen and merchant of York to John Watton chaplain and John Malton clerk of a messuage and moiety of a chamber, as in 445. Seal. Witnesses: as 444 (excluding Nicholas Blakburn, and with John Radclyff in place of Simon Waughen). York, 20 May 5 Henry V [1417]. SOURCE: VC 3/Vi 342 (311 mm. x 134 mm.) ENDORSEMENT: Carta Johannis Ward facta J. Walton et J. Malton de tenementis universis ubi J. Kyng manet iuxta ten' Nicholai Blakburn senioris undique. SEAL: as 444. NOTE: See 443.
