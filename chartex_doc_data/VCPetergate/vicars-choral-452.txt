vicars-choral-452
Grant by John Watton chaplain and John Malton clerk of York to Thomas Swanland, Robert Semer, George Whitteby, and William Lillyng de York, chaplains, of property, as in 450. Seal. Witnesses: Thomas del Gare mayor, John de Bolton, Thomas Davi, sheriffs, Henry de Preston, Thomas Esyngwald, Thomas Snawedon, Simon Waughen, and others. York, 4 July 8 Henry V [1420]. SOURCE: VC 3/Vi 347 (310 mm. x 126 mm.) ENDORSEMENT: Carta J. Watton et Johannis Mahon vicariis de tenementis in Petergat et Hornpotlane. SEALS: remains of two seals of red wax. NOTE: See 443.
