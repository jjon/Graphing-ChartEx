vicars-choral-455
Grant by Thomas Swanland, Robert Semer, George Whitteby, and William Lillyng de York, chaplains, to Richard Ulveskelfe warden of the house of vicars and the vicars of property, as in 450 Seal. Witnesses: as 452 (reading Gar for Gare, Davy for Davi, and Snawdon for Snawedon). York, 16 July 8 Henry V [1420]. SOURCE: VC I/I pp. 54-5 (ink pagination). NOTE: See 443.
