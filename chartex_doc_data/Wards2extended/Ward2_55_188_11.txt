Grant and quitclaim by John Whytman of Fordham, Essex, husbandman, to Robert Jenner of Stoke by Nayland, Suffolk, yeoman, of his field called 'Heard' comprising three and a half acres in Fordham That is to say between a tenement called Fordham Fryth on the east side and a tenement called Howdes on the west side and between the lands of Robert Potter called Fanes Land on the northern side and the lands of the said Robers Potter then in the tenure or occupation of John Spark on the southern side• Mark of John Whytman• Witnesses: John Fludd, John Rose and Richard Newton•
