Quitclaim by John Symond, son and heir of Walter Symonde, deceased, to John Mayston junior of Boxted, Essex, John Brett, William Kyng of Fordham, Essex, William Warde and John Marteyn of 'Leyham', of two pieces of arable land with appurtenances called 'Plomers' and 'Lynches' (details specified) in Fordham, Essex from which said parcel of land called Plomers extends itself from the green called Plomers Green on the eastern side to the lands called Stubbynges  on the western side• And the said piece of land called Lynches abuts the wood called Fordham Fryth on the eastern side and on the western side the king's highway called Holdersgreen, leading from the church of Fordham to the village of Wethermonford• Seal of John Symonde•
