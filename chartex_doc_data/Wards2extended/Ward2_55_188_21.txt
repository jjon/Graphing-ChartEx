Grant from Robert Kynge of West Bergholt, Essex, yeoman, to Richard Newton of Boxted, Essex, husbandman, of a messuage called 'Lyard', with a garden and a croft by estimation one acre and a half more or less in Fordham, Essex that is to say to the common there called Plummers Green towards the east and the messuage and lands now and lately of Robert Jenner towards the south and west and to the lands called Stubbings towards the north; as well as a field called Heards, by estimation three acres and a half more or less in Fordham, that is to say between the lands of the heirs of the late John Abell, knight, on the eastern side and the tenement called Howds on the western side, as well as a field called 'Heards', for a certain sum already paid• Seal and mark of Robert Kynge• [Endorsed] Sealed and seisin delivered in the presence of Robert Durrant, John Dyffett, Thomas Freman, George Armond, and Edmund Potter•
