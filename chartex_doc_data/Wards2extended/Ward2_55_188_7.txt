Quitclaim from John Gybbun (Gebon) of Fordham, Essex, yeoman, to Robert Jynnar (Jenner) of Fordham, bricklayer, of a croft of six acres of land in Fordham, in a field called 'Ballande' between the lands of Thomas Motanne called Chances on the north and the lands of John Pach on the south, one end abutting 'le Chancefenne' towards the west and the road leading from Potonbredge towards Yoyesbredge also towards the west• Dated Fordham, seal of John Gybbun• [Endorsed] witnesses: John Lucas, esquire, John Seman, Henry Park, George Burr and Jeronimi Balborow•
