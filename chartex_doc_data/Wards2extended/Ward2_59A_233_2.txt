Grant from Sir William Cecil, knight, lord Burghley, lord treasurer and master of the court of wards and liveries, and Thomas Seckford (Sekforde), esquire, surveyor of the liveries, for and on behalf of Elizabeth I, to Thomas Audley, gentleman, son and next heir of Thomas Audley, gentleman, deceased, of the livery of the lordship of the manor of Gosbecks, Essex, on condition of the declaration of the value and annual income of the manor [attached]• 14 February 1586 ii• Survey of the manor of Gosbecks, Essex, belonging to Thomas Audley, deceased, to be inherited by his son Thomas Audley• 2 April 1585
