Indenture of bargain and sale by Thomas Audley (Audeley) of Stanway, Essex, gentleman, to Edmund Markaunt of the parish of St Giles beside Colchester, Essex, gentleman, of a meadow called 'Chene Meadowe' or 'Horsse Meadowe' of 12 acres with appurtenances in the parish of Lexden, Essex, for £40, in consideration of other indentures between the parties concerning land in Colchester, Lexden and Myland, Essex, formerly belonging to the dissolved priory of Crutched Friars in Colchester• Dorse, witnesses: Richard Cabell, John Bodyham, Richard Newbold, Thomas Menley and Anthony Bannester• Seal of Edmund Markaunt•
