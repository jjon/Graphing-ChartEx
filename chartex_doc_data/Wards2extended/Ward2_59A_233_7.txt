Note of articles of agreement between Thomas Audley (Audeley), gentleman, and Roger Braberton, yeoman, regarding the lease of land called 'Chambers', part of the manor of Gosbecks [Essex], for the term of 7 years, at an annual rent of £5, on condition that Roger Braberton does not take any wood from the land without Thomas Audley's consent•
