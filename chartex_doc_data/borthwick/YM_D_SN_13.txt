YM/D/SN/13; Feoffment
John Mawdisley of Hirst Courtney, yeoman; Richard Doghtie• Moiety of a built toft in Snaith, with 1 rood land, lying together with the toft lately belonging to Robert Ruys to the east and that of John Elland to the west, abutting Snaith marsh to the north and the road to Dorr to the south, with all other possessions held freehold by charter within the soke of Snaith, and which he had of the gift and feoffment of Doghtie• Wit: Lionel Evers of Snaith, gent•, John Seriesson of the same, John White of the same, John Galyngby of Cowick•
