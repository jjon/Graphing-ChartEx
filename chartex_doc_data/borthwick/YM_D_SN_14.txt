YM/D/SN/14; Feoffment
Edmund Tyas of Pontefract, yeoman; John Atkynson and Richard Atkynson his son• An unbuilt toft in Lowgate, Balne, with five closes lying together and adjacent thereto, with the lands of Thomas Rikard of Hatfield to the east and west, Lowgate lane to the south, and Cat lane to the north• Wit: John Gaytingby, John Dykkyllpeney, James Pitte• £20 10s•
