YM/D/SN/17; Copy of court roll (manor of Snaith)
Admission of John Bawmbrugh following surrender made by George Watson• Toft and appurtenances in the east part of Snaith, near the tithe barn• Watson had been admitted tenant for a term of 60 years at the court held on 26 April, 2 Edward VI [1548], at annual rent of 10d• payable in equal parts at Martinmas and Whitsun, and attendance at the manorial court on the feasts of St Lawrence the martyr and St Michael the archangel• Bawmbrugh is admitted for the remainder of the term, on the same conditions• Fine of 10d•
