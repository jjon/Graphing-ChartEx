YM/D/SN/2; Acquittance
John Daunay; William de Burgoyn• Daunay had granted certain lands in Cowick and Pollington to Burgoyn, in return for a rose at the feast of St John the Baptist each year• Burgoyn is excused services for the lands• Wit: Thomas Dilcock, Richard Litster, Alexander Mangolf, John de Puknall•
