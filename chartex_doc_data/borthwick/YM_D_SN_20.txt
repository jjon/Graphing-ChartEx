YM/D/SN/20; Bond, to accept partition of an estate
Henry Skelton of Whitley, gent•, and Margaret Rycarde of Snaith, single woman; Edward Hirdde of Snaith, gent• Skelton and Elizabeth his wife, Rycarde, and Hirdde and Edith his wife, had appointed Thomas Jowland of Snaith, Edward Slingesby of the same, and Henry Motherby of Cowick, yeomen, to make a division of the property held within the soke of Snaith by Thomas Rycard of Snaith, deceased• The document setting out the award was to be delivered by Martinmas next• £200•
