YM/D/SN/21; Feoffment
Thomas Rickard of Snaith, gent•; John Slaydon of Pollington, yeoman• Close called the Great short croft, in Pollington, with the lands of Thomas Metham, esq•, to east and west, abutting a close called Brodcroft to the north and the lane called Wheat croft lane to the south, and now in Slaydon's tenure or occupation• Warranty of title against Rickard, Stephen Lutton, and Alice his wife• £10 5s• 0d•
