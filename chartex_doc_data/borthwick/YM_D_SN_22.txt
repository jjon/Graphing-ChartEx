YM/D/SN/22; Copy of court roll (manor of Snaith)
Admission of Edith Hirde, widow, daughter of Thomas Ricarde, deceased• 2 built tofts with appurtenances in Snaith, and 3 roods in the west field of Snaith, as allocated to her as her share of her father's possessions• Rent of 10s• per annum payable in equal parts at Michaelmas and Lady Day• Fine unrecorded• [A 17th century endorsement reads: `Copies of lands in Snaith purchased of Dorothea Holmes']•
