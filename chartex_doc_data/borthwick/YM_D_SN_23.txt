YM/D/SN/23; Feoffment
Brian Stapilton of Carlton, esq•; John Topclif of Pollington, bachelor• A built toft and the garden thereto adjacent, with the lands of John Topclif to the east and those of George Bawne to the north; 2 selions in Cowick field, with the lands of George Smithe to the east and those of Peter Alcocke to the west, and abutting the lands of Thomas Jowland to the south• The properties are to be held on an annual payment of 6d• to the crown• Stapilton appoints Richard Radcliff of Snaith to act as attorney•
