YM/D/SN/28; Feoffment
Richard Radcliff of Snaith, gent•; Walter Jobson of Brantingham, esq• ½ acre arable and pasture in Snaith, in a croft or garden lately belonging to Roger Phillippe and now in the tenure or occupation of Edmund Leighe, with the lands of the heirs of Thomas Dilcocke, gent•, to the west, and those of the heirs of Phillippe to the east, and abutting Dorgate to the south and Snaith marsh to the north• The lands were among those acquired by Radcliff by the transaction in YM/D/SN/27• Warranty of title•
