YM/D/SN/3; Feoffment
Margaret de Snayth, widow; Thomas Dylcock of Balne• Third part of a moiety of a toft or garden, lately the manor of Richard de Snayth, with the third part of a moiety of a dovecote therein, and the third part of the moiety of the base of a windmill in the west field of Snaith• Margaret had them of the gift and feoffment of Nichole, widow of Peter Baylyff of Snaith, John Camyn and Joan his wife, and Aveline Bladeworth• Wit: Thomas Crull, John Smyth, John Thornehyl, Richard Taverner of Snaith•
