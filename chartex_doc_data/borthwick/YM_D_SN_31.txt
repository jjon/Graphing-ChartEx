YM/D/SN/31; Bargain and sale
Mark Stapleton of Balne, yeoman; Robert Bawlne, yeoman and John his son• 2 closes called Cross Hill closes in Balen, with the lands of Thomas Mydleton to the west and Balne moor to the east, abutting the highway to the south and Balne moor to the north• The muniments to be handed over by Easter next• Stapleton is to retain seisin until the conveyance is completed, which shall be by Michaelmas• Covenants of unencumbrance, other than for an unexpired lease made to Robert Bawlne and John Eastwoode• Assurance of title by Stapleton and Elizabeth his wife for three years• £24•
