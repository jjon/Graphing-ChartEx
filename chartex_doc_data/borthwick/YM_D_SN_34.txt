YM/D/SN/34; Lease
Richard Ratclif of Rawcliffe, gent•; Nicholas Ricard, gent• A messuage or tenement, 1 acre land, 1 acre meadow, in Snaith, now or late in the tenure or occupation of Thomas Hembroughe; 3 lands in the west field of Snaith, between the close belonging to Ricard and the place called Gowdall stile, containing 1 acre• To be held for remainder of Ratclif's life at annual rent of 15s• 4d• payable in equal parts at Whitsun and Martinmas, or within 10 days thereafter• Covenant of peaceful possession•
