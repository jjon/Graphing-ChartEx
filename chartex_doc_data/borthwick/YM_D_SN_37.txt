YM/D/SN/37; Feoffment
Richard Radcliffe of Snaith, gent•; Walter Jobson of Brantingham, esq• Messuage in Snaith, with its buildings and the garden called the hemp garth adjacent thereto, now in the occupation of John Hornbye; ½ acre in the English moor, belonging to the messuage and in the occupation of John Topcliffe• Warranty of title• Radcliffe appoints as his attorneys William Headley of Snaith, merchant, and William Bradley of the same, yeoman•
