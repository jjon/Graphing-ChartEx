YM/D/SN/39; Bond, pursuant to Feoffment
Nicholas Porritt of Barlby, yeoman; Robert Bate of Gowdall, yeoman• Covenants for peaceful possession and unencumbrance of, 2 selions of land containing 1 acre, in the west field of Snaith and Cowick at Middle Oxney, with the lands of Thomas Dawnaye, esq•, to the east and those of George Lambert to the west, abutting the More land stye to the north and Nether Oxney to the south and of Porritt's right to dispose of them and assure title• £10•
