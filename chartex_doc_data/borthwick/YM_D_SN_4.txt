YM/D/SN/4; Feoffment
William Bakester and Robert Child of Pollington; Agnes, wife of John Benet of Pollington, senior• All lands, tenements, meadows, woods, pastures, etc•, which they had of the gift and feoffment of John Benet senior within the soke of Snaith• Alice is to hold the properties for life; if her husband survives her they are to pass to him and his heirs after her death• Wit: Richard Storr of Pollington and William his brother, John Benet of the same, junior, Thomas Pynder of the same, William Benet of the same•
