YM/D/SN/40; Memorandum of agreement
Stephen Boynton of Rawcliffe, yeoman; Edward Stanhope of Grays Inn, Middx•, esq• Boynton had sold 2½ acres meadow in Snaith ings, and promises to deliver a bounder of the lands before the next York assizes• Stanhope is to pay him £13 on 1 April next, or thereabouts•
