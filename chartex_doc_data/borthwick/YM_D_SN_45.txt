YM/D/SN/45; Bargain and sale
Thomas Fange of Balne croft, parish of Snaith, yeoman; Robert Mydlton of Fenwick, parish of Campsall, yeoman• Messuage and appurtenances with 5 closes adjoining, with the lands of Thomas Rickard, gent•, to east and west, abutting on Gayte lane to the south and Cat lane to the north, and with all the appurtenances in Lowgate in Balne• Deeds to be handed over by Easter next, by which date title to be completed• Covenants of unencumbrance and peaceful possession, with assurance of title by Fange and Anne his wife for three years• £67•
