YM/D/SN/6; Feoffment
Geoffrey Barker of Snaith and Isabella his wife; Richard Edmond of Snaith and John Osset• Moiety of a messuage at the west end of Snaith, with the messuage and garden of Edmond to the east, and the orchard of the heirs of Richard de Snayth to the west, with the common way through Snaith fields to Dorr on the south, and Snaith marsh on the north• Wit: Richard Dylcock, Thomas Dylcock senior, John Bubwith, John Wygan, John Thorp•
