YM/D/SN/7; Feoffment
Richard Edmunde of Snaith and John Osset of Cowick; John Rayner, John Dylcocke, and John Fox of Snaith• Lands as YM/D/SN/6 described as a curtilage and selion of land with its buildings, with Edmunde's curtilage to the east and the other part of the curtilage belonging to William Elande to the west, with the common way through Snaith fields to Dorr on the south, and Snaith marsh on the north• The lands had been given to them by Geoffrey Barker and Isabella his wife• Wit: Thomas Dylcoke, Thomas Gledowe, William Lighteshill•
