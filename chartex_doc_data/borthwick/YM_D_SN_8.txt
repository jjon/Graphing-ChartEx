YM/D/SN/8; Copy of court toll (manor of Pollington)
Admission of Robert Dylcock• 4 selions of land containing 3 acres and lying together in the west field of Snaith, with his own lands to the east and those of Robert Cap to the west, abutting on a toft of his to the north and on the road to Dorr to the south• To be held by Dylcock and the heirs male of his body at 3s• 4d• per annum payable in equal parts at Whitsun and Michaelmas• Fine of 3s• 4d•
