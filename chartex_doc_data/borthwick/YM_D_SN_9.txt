YM/D/SN/9; Feoffment
Thomas Mortymer of Balne, yeoman, and Alice his wife; John Brokden, yeoman `of the crown'• A toft in Lowgate, Balne, with four adjacent closes, abutting Lowgate lane to the south, and Cat lane to the north, and with the lands of Thomas Rikard of Hatfield to east and west• Wit: William Wyndle, Robert Rykard, Thomas Suger of Snaith, John Adam of Balne, Ralph Wood of Pollington•
