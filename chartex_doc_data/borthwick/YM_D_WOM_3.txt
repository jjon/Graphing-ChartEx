YM/D/WOM/3
Petition of Peter Standley of Womersley, esq•, and dame Margaret his wife (formerly wife of Sir William Gascoigne, kt•, deceased), to the Queen and the Council of the North• Sets out that, among other things, Gascoigne had granted a close in Womersley (now in the occupation of Nicholas Skeltone) to John Gascoigne, esq•, Thomas Wright, gent•, and others, as trustees for his wife• She had married Standley after Gascoigne's death, and was entitled to the lands, but Skeltone had invaded the property, and had occupied it for the past three years, to the damage of the petitioners of £20• They seek redress•
