Petition of Peter Standley of Womersley, esq•, to the Queen and the Council of the North• Sets out that Standley was the rightful owner of a close in Womersley called Pitcroft or Sherewood close, but somehow George Mydleton of Whitby had gained actual possession, and refused to yield it up, to Standley's damage of £100• He seeks redress•
