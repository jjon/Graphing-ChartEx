YM/D/WOM/6; Bargain and sale
Anthony Collins of London, esq•; William Stanley, son and heir apparent of Peter Stanley of Womersley, esq• Close called Sherewod close or Pit croft in Womersley, containing 8 acres, now in the occupation of Peter Stanley, with all appurtenances as fully as they were included in Letters Patent dated 26 July, 1585, by which the lands had been granted to Collins and Laurence Wodnett, deceased• To be held of the crown, in fealty only, as of the manor of East Greenwich, in return for payment of 2s• per annum in equal parts at Michaelmas and Lady Day•
