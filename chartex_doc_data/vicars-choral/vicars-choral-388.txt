Grant by Peter son of John Stoile to Richard the chaplain succentor of York of land with buildings in Petergate, lying in length and breadth between land once of Nicholas de Bugthorpe and that once of Martin Grandeth; paying Peter and his heirs id• at Christmas and the heirs of Emma daughter of Robert Grandeth 12d• [1226 x 1235] Sciant omnes presentes et futuri quo ego Petrus filius Johannis Stoile dedi, concessi, et hac carta sigillo meo impressa confirmavi Ricardo capellano succentori Ebor' ecclesie pro servicio suo totam terram mearn cum edificiis et aliis pertinenciis suis in Petergate sicuti iacet longitudine et latitudine inter terram que fuit Nicholai de Bugetorp' et terram que fuit Martini Grandeth, habendam et tenendam eidem Ricardo et eius assignatis de me et heredibus meis in feodo et hereditate, libere, quiete, pacifice, et integre cum omnibus pertinenciis, aisiamentis, et libertatibus suis sine aliquo retenemento; reddendo inde annuatim tresdecim denarios, milli scilicet et heredibus meis unum denarium ad natale Domini et heredibus Emme filie Roberti Grantdeth duodecim denarios pro me et heredibus meis, medietatem ad Pentecosten et medietatem ad festum sancti Martini in hyeme• Ego enim Petrus attornavi pro me et heredibus meis dictum Ricardum et assignatos suos ad dictam firmam solvendam in perpetuum• Et ego Petrus et heredes mei warantizabimus et defendemus ditto Ricardo et eius assignatis predictam terram cum pertinenciis per predictam servicium contra omnes in perpetuum• Hiis testibus Hugone de Seleby tune maiore civitatis Ebor', Alexandra filio Radulphi, Ada Flur, Willelmo Turkill', Ricardo dispensatore, Waltero filio Nicholai, Waltero de Roma, Radulpho Caule, Henrico de capella, Galfrido de Ottele, Thoma aurifabro, Simone longo, J• albo, Roaldo, et Reginaldo, capellanis, et aliis• SOURCE: VC 3/Vi 314 (150 mm• x 115 mm•) 'ENDORSEMENT: Carta Petri filiiJohannis Stoile;
