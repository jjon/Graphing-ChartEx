Grant, in free alms, by Richard son of Roger de Ouseburn chaplain, succentor of York, for the salvation of his soul and the souls of his parents and benefactors and all the faithful, to God, blessed Peter of York, and the vicars of land held of Peter Stoil son of John Stoil in Petergate, lying between the land which William Fairfax then held and that which John Blinsleg held• [later 1220s or 1230S] Universis' sancte matris ecclesie filiis ad quos •presens scriptum pervenerit Ricardus filius Rogeri de Useborne capellanus tune temporis succentor Ebor' ecclesie eternam in Domino salutem• Noverit universitas vestra me divine pietatis intuitu caritatis affectum et pro salute anime mee, parentum, benefactorum rneorum etanimarum omnium fidelium dedisse, concessisse, et hac presenti carta sigillo meo impressa confirmasse Deo et beato Petri Ebor' et vicariis ibidem Deo servientibus totam terram meam in Petergat' cum omnibus pertinenciis quam teneo de Petro Stoll filio Johannis Stoil que scilicet terra iacet inter terram quam Willelmus Fairfax tune tenuit et terram quam Johannes Blinsleg tune tenuit, tenendam et habendam dictis vicariis in liberam et perpetuum elemosinam inperpetuum sicut aliqua elemosina liberius et melius dari potest• Hiis testibus• SOURCE: VC 3/1/1, fa• 46v• Initial U omitted as later illumination in MS• was intended• DATE: After 388• NOTE: See 388•
