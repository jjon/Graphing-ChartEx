Final concord made in the Court of Common Pleas by Robert Brudenell, knight, and other justices, in dispute between Robert Jenner (Jenour), bricklayer (brykleyer), John Brett, John Bredge, Walter Kyng, Thomas Pechye, plaintiffs, and Richard Duke, defendant, concerning a messuage, garden and 12 acres of land in Fordham, Essex• Dated at Westminster• Witnesses: Robert Brudenell
