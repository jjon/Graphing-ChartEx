Indenture by which Thomas Audley (Audeley) of Essex, gentleman, and Beatrice, his wife, leased to William Watson of London, gentleman, a messuage called the Crutched Friars, with barns, stables, houses and pasture with appurtenances, enclosed with a brick wall, in Colchester, Essex, for the term of 21 years, at an annual rent of a red rose on 24 June if it be demanded• An additional lease is attached relating to the mansion house called the Crutched Friars•
