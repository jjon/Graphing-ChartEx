Indenture by which Beatrice Audley (Audeley) of Gosbecks in Stanway, Essex, widow, to Thomas Audley (Audeley), her son, of Stanway, Essex, gentleman, of 15 acres of land called the 'Comon Fylde' in Stanway, Essex, in the manor of Gosbecks, and the feeding pasture for 100 sheep called 'Stanwaye Heathe', for the term of 10 years, for annual rent of 20s• Witnesses: Peter Whelcombe and Richard Tunstall• Dorse, note that the lease was cancelled•
