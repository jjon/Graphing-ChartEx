Grant of a lease from Beatrice Audley (Audeley) of Gosbecks in Stanway, Essex, widow, and Thomas Audley (Audeley), gentleman, her son, to Edward Wybarowe and Stephen Boulton of Berechurch, Essex, yeomen, of the rights and interests in the pasture for 100 sheep called 'Stanway Heathe' and the field called the 'Comon Feld' in Stanway, Essex, [location specified], for the term of 7 years, for annual rent of 26s 8d• Witnesses: Peter Whelcombe and John Madison• Dorse: note that the lease was cancelled• Signet seals of Edward Wybarowe and Stephen Boulton•
