Indenture by which Thomas Audley (Audeley) of Gosbecks, Essex, gentleman, leases to Richard Plomer of Layer-de-la-Haye, Essex, yeoman, the mansion house called Gosbecks with all its buildings, barns, stables, and gardens with appurtenances, and pieces of land [location specified], with exceptions, for the term of 5 years, for annual rent of £23• Witnesses: Thomas Audeley and Andrew Asshe• Seal of Richard Plomer•
